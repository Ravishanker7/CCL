Sleep Wellness

Tips for Better Sleep

A great mattress is just the start. Follow these expert-backed habits to wake up refreshed every morning.

01

Keep a Sleep Schedule

Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day — even on weekends. Consistency trains your body's internal clock.

02

Cool Your Bedroom

The ideal sleep temperature is 18–22°C. A breathable mattress and light bedding help you stay cool through the night.

03

Limit Screen Time

Put away phones and screens at least 30 minutes before bed. Blue light suppresses melatonin and delays sleep onset.

04

Support Your Spine

Your mattress should keep your spine neutral — not sagging or too stiff. Replace mattresses every 8–10 years for best support.

+
Yes! Every Comfort Cave mattress comes with a 100-night free trial. If you're not completely satisfied within 100 nights, we'll collect the mattress and issue a full refund — no questions asked.
What is the warranty period?
+

Absolutely — visit our Shaikpet showroom to try all three mattress collections in person. Our sleep consultants will help you find the right firmness and size for your needs.
