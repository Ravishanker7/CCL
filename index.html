FAQ

Common Questions

Which mattress is best for back pain?
+
Our Ortho Support Mattress is doctor-recommended for back pain sufferers. It features a firm support layer with targeted pressure relief zones that maintain natural spine alignment throughout the night.
What is the warranty period?
+
All our mattresses come with a comprehensive 15-year warranty covering manufacturing defects, sagging beyond 1.5 inches, and structural damage under normal use conditions.
Can I try before I buy?
+
Absolutely — visit our Shaikpet showroom to try all three mattress collections in person. Our sleep consultants will help you find the right firmness and size for your needs.
What mattress size should I choose?
+
For solo sleepers, a Single or Double works well. Couples should opt for Queen or King for comfortable space. Visit our Mattress Guide section above for full dimensions and recommendations.
What is your return policy?
+
Once a mattress has been purchased and delivered, it cannot be returned or exchanged. Please ensure you select the correct size and model before placing your order. If you have any questions, our team will be happy to assist you before your purchase.
