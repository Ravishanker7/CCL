Warranty Policy & Return Guidelines

Pro-Rata Warranty

Your mattress is covered by a limited warranty for the specified warranty period.

How it works:

If a manufacturing defect occurs after the initial Free Replacement Period, the warranty will continue on a pro-rata basis. This means you will receive warranty support by paying only a proportion of the current product price based on the period of use.

  • During the initial free replacement period, eligible manufacturing defects are covered at no cost.
  • After the free replacement period ends, the replacement cost is calculated on a pro-rata basis according to the remaining warranty period.
  • The customer is responsible for paying the applicable pro-rata amount along with any transportation or handling charges, if applicable.

Example:

If your mattress comes with a 15-year warranty and a manufacturing defect occurs in the 6th year, you will only pay the applicable pro-rata amount as per the warranty policy instead of the full price of a new mattress.

Note: The warranty covers manufacturing defects only. It does not cover normal wear and tear, misuse, stains, burns, improper handling, or damage caused by an unsuitable bed base.

Return Policy

Once a mattress has been purchased and delivered, it cannot be returned or exchanged.
